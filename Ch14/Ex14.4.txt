Question: Explain how to decide whether the following should be class members: 
(a) % 
(b) %= 
(c) ++ 
(d) -> 
(e) << 
(f) && 
(g) == 
(h) ()

Answer:
(a): nonmember
(b): ought to be a member
(c): usually should be a member
(d): must be a memeber
(e): nonmember
(f): nonmember
(g): nonmember
(h): must be a member
